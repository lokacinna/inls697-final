Interviewing Michael


You turn your attention to Michael Scott in his office, and he immediately begins sinking his head into his hands. “Oh God. What if it was me? What if I’m the killer?”

You're pretty sure he would know if he was.

You assure Michael that the evidence doesn’t point to him.

He sighs. “Well, if it’s not me, then my next guess would be Toby. Maybe these security tapes will prove he’s guilty; I don’t know.” He hands you a couple of video tapes.

Finally, we’re getting somewhere!


Let's watch the security footage.
